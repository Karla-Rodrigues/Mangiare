THE RESTAURANT

Your neighbourhood pizza place in Toronto with fast delivery for all your pizza
needs. Mangiare Delivery is home to one of the best and oldest pizzeria’s in Toronto.
Be sure to grab a pizza next time you are in the St. Clair area.
Mangiare Delivery is family business that has been operated by the Benetti’s since 1992.
The two chefs at this restaurant are a dynamic duo of mother and son.
Manola and Paolo Benetti are the minds behind the classic Italian dishes they offer to
their clients. The restaurant atmosphere is warm and filled with friendly faces that are
a staple here. It is filled with aromas of freshly made dough and sizzling pizza.
